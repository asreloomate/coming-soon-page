About RelooMate

We're a youth-focused flat-sharing platform for India, helping college students and young professionals find compatible flatmates safely, affordably, and easily. We tackle high rent burdens, trust issues, and unstructured listings to empower young Indians to live better.

Our Vision

Empowering young Indians to live affordably, safely, and comfortably through seamless flat-sharing.

Who We Serve

College students, young professionals, and anyone relocating for internships or jobs.

The Problem

Solving the fragmented rental ecosystem burdened by high costs, lack of trust, and difficult searches.
